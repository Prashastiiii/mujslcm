OVERVIEW

Manipal University Jaipur Placements 2023 is concluded with 98% placements. The participating batch received 1497 placement offers. Manipal University Jaipur average package increased by about 7% to INR 7.50 LPA whereas the average package stood at INR 9 LPA for the Engineering students. More than 289 recruiters participated in Manipal University Placements 2023. Some of the top recruiters included Accenture, Cognizant, Capgemini, Deloitte, Amazon etc.
